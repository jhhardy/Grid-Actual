Forward Logistics Command Interface
Manage convoy assets across a contested operational grid. Maximize total score across 20 rounds.
Operational Briefing

You are managing resource allocation across a 10×10 grid of forward operating zones. Each zone has a demand level that determines how valuable a convoy deployment will be. You will complete 20 rounds, each comprising 10 decisions.

On each decision, select a zone. Clicking a scouted, eligible zone deploys a convoy immediately. Clicking an unknown zone or a blocked zone opens a decision panel where you choose between the two actions:

▸ Deploy convoy
Commit resources
Send a supply convoy. Points awarded based on the zone's current demand level at time of deployment.
+5 to +50 pts · scaled to demand
▸ Send scout
Gather intelligence
Dispatch a reconnaissance unit. Reveals the zone's demand level. Intelligence is retained in future rounds.
+8 pts · demand confirmed
Convoy movement: The first deployment each round may target any zone. After that, your convoy may only move to zones adjacent (including diagonal) to its current position. Scouting is always unrestricted.

Zone demand levels are hidden until a scout is sent. Scouting history persists across all rounds.
